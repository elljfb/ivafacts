## How to Use These Tools

### 1. Start with Eligibility
If you're new to IVAs, begin with the **Eligibility Calculator** to see if you meet the basic requirements.

### 2. Estimate Your Payment
Use the **Monthly Payment Calculator** to understand what you might afford to pay toward an IVA.

### 3. Compare Options
Try the **Debt Solution Comparator** to see how an IVA compares to other options based on your circumstances.

### 4. Understand Costs
Finally, use the **Cost Breakdown** tool to see where your payments would go.

## What Happens Next?

After using these calculators:

1. **If IVA seems suitable**: Research licensed Insolvency Practitioners
2. **If uncertain**: Read our [comparison guides](/debt-solutions/)
3. **For more information**: Explore our [FAQ section](/faq/)
4. **For professional advice**: Contact free debt charities:
   - StepChange: 0800 138 1111
   - National Debtline: 0808 808 4000
   - Citizens Advice: Local bureau
