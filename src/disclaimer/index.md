## Website Disclaimer

### Information Only - Not Financial Advice

IVA Facts is an educational resource providing general information about Individual Voluntary Arrangements (IVAs) and related debt solutions in the United Kingdom. **We do not provide financial advice.**

### What We Provide

- General information about IVAs and debt solutions
- Educational content to help you understand your options
- Comparison tools and calculators for estimation purposes
- Links to licensed professionals and debt charities

### What We Don't Provide

- ❌ Personal financial advice
- ❌ Recommendations to enter specific debt solutions
- ❌ IVA setup or administration services
- ❌ Credit counseling or debt management services
- ❌ Legal advice

### Not Licensed Practitioners

**Important**: IVA Facts and its operators are not licensed Insolvency Practitioners. We cannot:

- Set up IVAs
- Act as nominees or supervisors
- Provide regulated financial advice
- Make formal recommendations about your debt situation

### Always Seek Professional Advice

Before making any decisions about debt solutions, you must:

1. **Consult a licensed Insolvency Practitioner** if considering an IVA
2. **Seek free advice** from regulated debt charities such as:
   - StepChange Debt Charity
   - National Debtline
   - Citizens Advice
   - Money Helper

3. **Review your situation** with a qualified financial advisor where appropriate

### Accuracy of Information

We strive to ensure all information is accurate and up-to-date. However:

- Debt legislation and regulations can change
- Individual circumstances vary greatly
- Calculator results are estimates only
- Information should not be relied upon without verification

## Regulatory Information

### Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)

Debt advice in the UK is regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority. Licensed debt advisors must be authorized by the FCA.

To verify an advisor's credentials, visit: [FCA Register](https://register.fca.org.uk/)

### Insolvency Practitioners Association (IPA)

Insolvency Practitioners must be licensed by a recognized professional body. To find licensed IPs, visit:

- [Find an Insolvency Practitioner](https://www.gov.uk/find-an-insolvency-practitioner)

### Your Rights

Under UK consumer protection laws, you have rights when dealing with financial service providers. For more information:

- [Financial Ombudsman Service](https://www.financial-ombudsman.org.uk/)
- [Money Helper](https://www.moneyhelper.org.uk/)
