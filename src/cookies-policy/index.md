## Cookie Policy

**Last Updated**: January 12, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how IVA Facts uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit ivafacts.co.uk.

### What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites:

- Remember your preferences
- Understand how you use the site
- Improve your user experience
- Display relevant content and advertisements

### How We Use Cookies

IVA Facts uses cookies for:

1. **Essential Website Function**: Enable core features
2. **Analytics**: Understand website usage and performance
3. **Advertising**: Display relevant advertisements
4. **User Preferences**: Remember your settings

### Types of Cookies We Use

#### 1. Strictly Necessary Cookies

**Purpose**: Essential for website operation  
**Duration**: Session or up to 12 months  
**Can be disabled**: No (required for site function)

These cookies enable:
- Basic website navigation
- Secure access to certain features
- Cookie consent preferences

#### 2. Analytics Cookies

**Provider**: Google Analytics  
**Purpose**: Understand website usage  
**Duration**: Up to 26 months  
**Can be disabled**: Yes

Information collected:
- Pages visited
- Time spent on site
- Traffic sources
- Device and browser type
- Geographic location (city/country level)

**Opt-out**: [Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

#### 3. Advertising Cookies

**Provider**: Google AdSense  
**Purpose**: Display relevant advertisements  
**Duration**: Up to 24 months  
**Can be disabled**: Yes

These cookies:
- Remember websites you visit
- Enable targeted advertising
- Measure ad effectiveness
- Control ad frequency

**Manage preferences**: [Google Ad Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)

#### 4. Functionality Cookies

**Purpose**: Remember your preferences  
**Duration**: Up to 12 months  
**Can be disabled**: Yes

These cookies remember:
- Calculator inputs (stored locally)
- Language preferences
- Display settings

### Managing Cookies

#### Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
- **Firefox**: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
- **Edge**: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

**Note**: Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

#### Cookie Preference Center

You can manage your cookie preferences using our cookie consent banner that appears on your first visit. To change preferences later, click the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer.

### Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services:

#### Google Analytics
- Privacy Policy: [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- Opt-out: [tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

#### Google AdSense
- Privacy Policy: [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- Ad Settings: [google.com/settings/ads](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)

We do not control third-party cookies. Please review their privacy policies for more information.

### Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers offer "Do Not Track" signals. Currently, there is no industry standard for responding to DNT. We do not alter our data collection practices when we receive DNT signals.

### Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use:

- **Local Storage**: Store data locally in your browser
- **Session Storage**: Temporary storage cleared when you close your browser
- **Pixels/Beacons**: Track website usage and ad performance

These technologies serve similar purposes to cookies and are managed through browser settings.
