## Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 12, 2026

IVA Facts ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit ivafacts.co.uk.

### Information We Collect

#### Information You Provide

When you use our calculators or contact forms, you may provide:

- Financial information (debt amounts, income, expenses)
- Contact information (name, email address)
- Demographic information (employment status, location)

**Note**: Calculator data is processed locally in your browser and is not stored on our servers unless you explicitly submit a contact form.

#### Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring website
- Device information

### How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide Services**: Process calculator inputs and display results
2. **Improve Website**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
3. **Communicate**: Respond to inquiries submitted through contact forms
4. **Analytics**: Understand website traffic and popular content
5. **Advertising**: Display relevant advertisements (Google AdSense)

### Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data under:

- **Consent**: When you voluntarily provide information via forms
- **Legitimate Interests**: To operate and improve our website
- **Legal Obligations**: To comply with UK data protection laws

### Cookies

IVA Facts uses cookies to enhance your experience. See our [Cookie Policy](/cookies-policy/) for detailed information.

### Third-Party Services

#### Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand website usage. Google Analytics collects:

- Pages visited
- Time on site
- Geographic location (country/city level)
- Device and browser information

Google Analytics data is anonymized. You can opt-out using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

#### Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history. You can control ad personalization through [Google Ad Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

### Data Retention

- **Calculator Data**: Not stored (processed in browser only)
- **Contact Form Submissions**: Retained for 12 months
- **Analytics Data**: Retained for 26 months (Google Analytics default)
- **Cookies**: As specified in our Cookie Policy

### Your Rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to:

1. **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate personal data
3. **Erasure**: Request deletion of your personal data
4. **Restriction**: Limit how we use your data
5. **Portability**: Receive your data in a structured format
6. **Object**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
7. **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us via our [Contact page](/contact/).

### Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- HTTPS encryption for data transmission
- Secure hosting infrastructure
- Regular security updates
- Limited access to personal data
- No storage of sensitive financial data

However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

### Children's Privacy

IVA Facts is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us immediately.

### International Data Transfers

Our website is hosted in the UK/EU. Some third-party services (e.g., Google) may transfer data internationally. These services comply with appropriate data protection safeguards.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date". Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.
